Prusa Live #1 - An Experiment

The first in a series of Podcasts from Prusa Research. A company made famous for creating the Original Prusa 3D Printers

Glitched - A VR Podcast

Glitched speaks with Joel Telling - The 3D Printing Nerd about how he started his career into 3D printing, how he quit working at Adobe to pursue YouTube full time and the importance of sleep.

3D Prinitng in Education

Suz Somersall of KiraKira has created an online platform for girls to develop their 3D modeling skills by creating free courses and curriculum for 3D print STEAM education. KiraKira provides lessons and projects that ignite and inspire young girls to work on projects in what is widely a male dominated area of learning.

Prusa Live #4 - NERF BLASTERS

Joined by special guest - Luke from OutOfDarts! the Prusa team talked about 3D printed Nerf blasters and mods, Luke's 3D printing farm made of Original Prusa printers, how he manages it, what materials he uses and how 3D printing enabled him incredibly fast prototyping.
